# WHOOP Health Dashboard

A personal health dashboard built with vanilla JavaScript that pulls your
real WHOOP data. Connect once, then explore your recovery, sleep, and
strain all in one place.

---

## What It Does

Authenticate with your WHOOP account and get a live view of your recovery
scores, sleep stage breakdowns, HRV trends, and workout history — with
charts, filters, and search built in.

---

## How to Use

1. Click "Connect WHOOP" and log in with your WHOOP account via OAuth
2. Your dashboard loads automatically with today's recovery, strain, and sleep
3. Browse the 30-day trend charts for HRV, resting heart rate, and recovery
4. Filter sleep data by date range or search your workout log by sport type
